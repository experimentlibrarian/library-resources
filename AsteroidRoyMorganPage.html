📊 Asteroid – Roy Morgan Database

Everything you need to access and use the Asteroid database for your marketing research assignment.

📌 What is Asteroid and why do I need it? ▸

Asteroid is a desktop application that lets you explore survey data about New Zealand consumers — what they buy, where they shop, their demographics, and lifestyles.

For your MKTG601 assignment, you will use Asteroid to build cross-tabulation tables that link consumer groups to your chosen brand or product category.

💡 This page helps with the tool, not the assignment The chatbot and videos below show you how to operate Asteroid. For help choosing your brand, writing objectives, or writing your report — talk to your lecturer or TA.

💬 Ask the Asteroid chatbot ▸

Type a question and get a plain-English, step-by-step answer. The chatbot can help you open Asteroid, find variables, build tables, and understand your data.

💡 The chatbot cannot see your screen or write your assignment It knows how the software works. If it is unsure, it will say so.

📚 Other marketing databases ▸
⚠️ Researching a grocery or FMCG product? Asteroid does not hold grocery sales data. Use the Nielsen database instead — ask a librarian for help.
Nielsen
Retail sales data for grocery and FMCG products in NZ. Find on the library website.
Passport (Euromonitor)
Market sizes, company shares, and industry forecasts. Find on the library website.
Mintel
Consumer trends, brand analysis, and attitudes reports. Find on the library website.
